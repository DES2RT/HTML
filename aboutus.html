About the Chord Spinners

We are a dynamic group of women who love to sing four-part harmony in the barbershop style! We became an official member of Harmony Incorporated in 1982.

Harmony Incorporated was founded in 1959 and has members throughout Canada and the US. The Chord Spinners are strong advocates of Harmony Inc's mission - singing a cappella harmony definitely makes our spirits soar and we've found our voice!.

We practice every Monday night from 7:00 - 9:30 pm at the St James Lutheran church in St Jacobs. Members travel from Kitchener, Waterloo, Guelph, St Jacobs, Acton, Baden, New Hamburg and Wellesley..

What brings us together is the love of singing. What keeps us together is passion, growth, and unforgettable friendships.
