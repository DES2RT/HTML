Join the Chord Spinners

We would be absolutely thrilled to share our passion for singing with you! We are an educational organization that embraces continuous learning through weekly practices, guest coaches, workshops, learning CDs, our talented director and membership support.

If you have some talent and simply love singing, come and see what we're about. The vast benefits of choral singing are well documented and you'll experience the benefits as soon as you walk into our practice hall.

If you're interested, simply Contact Us and a member of our chorus will reply explaining how to best join our chorus. If you wish to pursue becoming a member, we'll guide you through the process.
